Welcome to Monitor Glue
Monitor Glue keeps your windows glued to the right monitor — and puts them back when you reconnect a display it recognizes.
One permission: Accessibility
It lets Monitor Glue read and move other apps' windows — the only thing it needs to restore your layout.
Monitor Glue never reads window contents — only their size and position.
How it works · Arrange your windows once. Reconnect a monitor it knows, and they snap back automatically.

Menu-bar icon
in the menu bar
A monochrome template glyph — a display with a pin/anchor through it. As a template image it inherits the menu-bar tint, so it reads correctly in light and dark menu bars and when highlighted.
App icon
Full-color squircle for the Finder / About box. The same glyph, reversed white on the brand blue. (No Dock icon — the app runs as a menu-bar accessory.)
